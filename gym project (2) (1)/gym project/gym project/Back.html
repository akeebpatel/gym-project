BACK

1) Grab The Bar.Grip it about shoulder-width apart.Full grip with your palms down.
2) Hang. Raise your feet off the floor by bending your knees.Hang with straight arms.
3) Pull. Pull yourself up by pulling your elbows down to the floor. Keep your elbowsclose.
4) Pass The bar. Pull yourself all the way up until your chin passes the bar. Don't
do half reps.
5)Lower yourself all the way down until your arms are straight. Breathe.Pullup again.

Benefits of Back Exercises :-
1. Latissimus dorsi, the large muscles that originate below the armpits and run down the back
of the ribcage and are responsible for shoulder movement
2.Rhomboids, muscles located in the mid-upper back that are responsible for retraction of your
scapula, or shoulder blades
3.Erector spinae, muscles that run along your spine and control extension and lateral movement
